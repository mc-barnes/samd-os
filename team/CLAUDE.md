# Team

Onboarding, retrospectives, cross-functional decisions, and sources of truth.

## Doc index
- onboarding/   — New team member onboarding checklists and role-specific guides.
- retros/       — Sprint and project retrospective records.
- decisions/    — Cross-functional decision records spanning multiple domains.
- sources-of-truth.md — Canonical location registry for authoritative facts.

## Key context
Onboarding for regulated product teams must include training on the quality management system, SDLC process, and design control procedures. Track training completion as a controlled record.

## Not in this folder
- Product decisions (→ product/strategy/)
- Regulatory documents (→ regulatory/)
- Engineering specs (→ engineering/rfcs/)
Cross-functional decisions that span multiple domains live in team/decisions/.
