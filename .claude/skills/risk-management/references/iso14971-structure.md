# ISO 14971 Risk Management Process

## Process Phases (Clauses 3-10)

| Clause | Phase | Key Activities |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| 4 | Risk Analysis | Intended use, hazard identification, risk estimation |
| 5 | Risk Evaluation | Compare estimated risk to acceptability criteria |
| 6 | Risk Control | Select measures, verify effectiveness, assess residual |
| 7 | Overall Residual Risk | Evaluate acceptability of combined residual risks |
| 8 | Risk Mgmt Report | Summarize process, traceability, conclusions |
| 9 | Production/Post-Production | Monitor field data, update analysis |

## Required Documents
1. Risk Management Plan -- scope, acceptability criteria, verification activities
2. Risk Management File -- living folder containing all risk records
3. Risk Management Report -- summary with overall residual risk conclusion

## Key Definitions
- **Hazard**: Potential source of harm (e.g., incorrect SpO2 reading)
- **Hazardous Situation**: Circumstance where people/property exposed to hazard
- **Harm**: Physical injury or damage to health (includes delayed treatment)
- **Sequence of Events**: Chain from hazard -> hazardous situation -> harm
- **Risk**: Combination of probability of harm and severity of that harm

## Relationship to IEC 62304
- SW safety class derived from risk analysis (not the other way around)
- Risk controls that are implemented in software -> SW requirements
- SW anomaly assessment feeds back into risk analysis
