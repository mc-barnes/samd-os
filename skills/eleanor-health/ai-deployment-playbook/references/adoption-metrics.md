# AI Adoption Metrics & KPIs

## Purpose

Defines measurable KPIs for tracking AI adoption success across internal and member-facing deployments. Includes healthcare-specific benchmarks where available.

## Internal AI Adoption Metrics (Claude Enterprise)

### Usage Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Daily Active Users (DAU) | Unique employees using AI tools per day | 60% of licensed users within 90 days | Tool analytics dashboard |
| Weekly Active Users (WAU) | Unique employees per week | 80% of licensed users within 90 days | Tool analytics dashboard |
| Sessions per User per Week | Average AI sessions per active user | 5+ sessions/week | Tool analytics |
| Use Case Diversity | Number of distinct use case categories per user | 3+ categories within 60 days | Survey + analytics |

### Productivity Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Time Saved per User per Week | Self-reported hours saved by using AI | 3+ hours/week | Monthly survey |
| Task Completion Rate | % of AI-assisted tasks completed successfully | >85% | User feedback + sampling |
| Document Drafting Speed | Time to produce first draft (with vs. without AI) | 40% reduction | Before/after comparison |
| Meeting Prep Time | Time to prepare for meetings using AI summarization | 30% reduction | Survey |

### Quality Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Error Reduction | Errors in AI-assisted vs. manual workflows | 20% reduction | QA sampling |
| Revision Cycles | Number of revision rounds for AI-drafted documents | <2 rounds on average | Document tracking |
| User Satisfaction (NPS) | Net Promoter Score for AI tools | NPS >30 at 90 days | Quarterly survey |

## Member-Facing AI Metrics (Voice/SMS Agents)

### Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Interaction Completion Rate | % of AI interactions completed without human handoff | >70% for routine tasks | Call/SMS analytics |
| Opt-Out Rate | % of members who opt out of AI interaction | <15% | Opt-out tracking |
| Member Satisfaction (CSAT) | Post-interaction satisfaction score | >4.0/5.0 | Post-call/SMS survey |
| Response Time | Average time to first AI response | <5 seconds (voice), <30 seconds (SMS) | System monitoring |

### Operational Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Call Deflection Rate | % of calls handled by AI without human agent | >40% for eligible call types | Call routing analytics |
| Scheduling Conversion | % of AI scheduling interactions that result in a booked appointment | >60% | Scheduling system |
| Lead Conversion Rate | % of outbound AI contacts that convert to scheduled intake | >15% (vs. manual baseline) | CRM tracking |
| Average Handle Time | Duration of AI-handled interactions | <3 min (voice), <5 messages (SMS) | Interaction analytics |

### Safety Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Crisis Escalation Accuracy | % of crisis situations correctly identified and escalated | 100% (zero tolerance for misses) | Transcript review |
| False Positive Rate | % of non-crisis interactions incorrectly escalated | <10% | Escalation log review |
| Time-to-Human (Crisis) | Time from crisis detection to human connection | <60 seconds (Tier 1) | System monitoring |
| Scope Violations | Instances where AI exceeded practice boundaries | 0 per month | Transcript audit |

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Audience | Frequency | Content |
|--------|----------|-----------|---------|
| AI Dashboard | All stakeholders | Real-time | Usage metrics, uptime, error rates |
| Adoption Report | ELT | Monthly | Adoption %, productivity gains, ROI |
| Safety Report | Clinical leadership | Monthly | Escalation accuracy, scope violations, adverse events |
| ROI Analysis | ELT + Board | Quarterly | Cost savings, revenue impact, member outcomes |
| Compliance Report | Compliance team | Quarterly | PHI incidents, policy violations, audit findings |
