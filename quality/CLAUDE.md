# Quality

## Doc index
- capa/         — Corrective and Preventive Action records. Per ISO 13485 Clause 8.5.2/8.5.3.
- complaints/   — Customer complaint records and trend analyses. Per ISO 13485 Clause 8.2.2.
- audits/       — Internal and external audit findings, responses, and closure tracking.

## Key context
CAPA and complaint records are controlled documents — always include root cause analysis and effectiveness verification. Complaints that involve patient harm require MDR filing (see analytics/post-market/).
